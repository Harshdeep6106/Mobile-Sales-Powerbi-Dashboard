# Mobile-Sales-Dashboard (Powerbi)
This Mobile Sales Dashboard is designed to be presented to sales managers, regional heads, business analysts, and senior management. It helps them monitor sales performance, identify top-selling brands and models, analyze city-wise sales, track customer ratings, and understand payment method trends for better business decision-making.

🎯 Objectives
The objective of this Mobile Sales Dashboard is to analyze mobile sales data and provide clear insights into total sales, quantity sold, transactions, average price, and customer ratings.

It helps identify top-performing mobile brands, models, cities, and payment methods so that sales managers and business teams can make better data-driven decisions.

The dashboard also tracks month-to-date performance and compares current sales with the same period last year to understand sales growth and business trends.
